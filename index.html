About me

Dr. Katherine Holland is an active teacher and freelance bassoonist in the Atlanta area. She currently serves on faculty at Mercer University. In addition, she is the Executive Director and a co-founder of the Atlanta Chamber Music Festival.


She is the Principal Bassoonist of the LaGrange Symphony Orchestra and bassoonist for Atlanta Chamber Winds. Prior to relocating to Atlanta, she served as the Interim Principal Bassoon of the Knoxville Symphony and Knoxville Opera.


She has played with many groups in the area and beyond including: Charleston Symphony, Charleston Opera,Atlanta Symphony Orchestra, Greenville Symphony Orchestra, Atlanta Ballet, Asheville Symphony, Orquesta Sinfonica UANL (Mexico), Macon Symphony, Orchestra Nashville, and Nashville Opera. In addition, she has toured both within the United States,Japan, and China and has participated in the Bang On a Can Festival (NY), the Rossini Festival (TN), and the Hamptons Choral Society Festival (NY).


As a chamber musician, Dr. Holland has performed with Anon Ensemble, Mercury Season Players(GA), Classical Music Revolution (GA), Morningside Chamber Players (GA), Astralis Wind Quintet, North Shore Pro Musica Society (NY), Synergy Winds (NY), Prime Ensemble (NY), TACTUS Ensemble (NY).


As a soloist, she won two concerto competitions in Tennessee as an undergraduate resulting in performances of the C.M. Weber Concerto in F Major and the Vivaldi Concerto in e minor. In addition, she performs solo and chamber recitals regularly and has appeared most recently as a guest soloist with the Mercer University Flute Choir.


Dr. Holland is a strong advocate of music education and began her formal studies earning a B.M. in Music Education from Tennessee Tech University with Jim Lotz. She went on to study performance with Frank Morelli at Stony Brook University where she earned an M.M. and D.M.A. She maintains an active clinician schedule in addition to her private studio teaching. She has been published in The Instrumentalist and the IDRS journal.

Private Lesson Details:

I teach private lessons, reed lessons, masterclasses, and sectionals throughout Atlanta and the surrounding areas. Please contact me for specific inquiries and for my teaching location closest to you.

My students range from beginners to adults and from middle school through adult.
